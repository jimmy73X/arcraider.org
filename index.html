Welcome to the Front Lines

Welcome to the front lines of humanity's last stand. ARC Raiders is a free-to-play, cooperative third-person shooter where you and your squad of Raiders fight to survive a relentless mechanized threat from orbit. Earth has been silenced, its future hanging in the balance. As a Raider, you are an unlikely hero, forged in the ruins of our world, ready to fight back against the overwhelming force of ARC.

The World

A once-familiar world lies in beautiful decay. The skies, once a symbol of freedom, are now a conduit for destruction, raining down a mysterious and unforgiving enemy known as ARC. As a Raider, you will navigate stunning yet dangerous landscapes—from forgotten urban sprawls to desolate coastlines—each holding secrets of the past and the key to our future.

The Threat - ARC

ARC is an automated, merciless invasion force of colossal machines. Their origin and purpose are unknown, but their directive is clear: eradicate all forms of resistance. They are adaptive, persistent, and operate with a hive-like intelligence. From agile hunter-killers to colossal behemoths, you must learn their patterns, exploit their weaknesses, and work together to bring them down.

Become a Raider

You are not a soldier. You are a survivor, a scavenger, an innovator. The Raiders are a disparate group of individuals, united by a common goal: to live free and fight back. Utilize a dynamic sandbox of weapons, gadgets, and the environment itself to outsmart your enemy. Your wits, agility, and cooperation are your greatest weapons.

How to Play ARC Raiders

A Guide to Surviving the ARC Threat

Squad Up. Fight Smart. Survive the Onslaught.

The Core Loop: Cooperation and Chaos

ARC Raiders is a free-to-play, third-person, cooperative shooter where teamwork and adaptability are your keys to survival. The core gameplay loop revolves around forming a squad, deploying into a hostile open world, completing objectives, engaging massive ARC machines, and extracting against all odds.

Before the Drop: Assemble Your Squad and Loadout

Success starts before you even set foot on the battlefield.

  • Form Your Fireteam: Team up with friends or matchmake with other players. A coordinated squad of 2-3 Raiders is essential for tackling the challenges ahead.
  • Choose Your Tools: Customize your loadout. This includes selecting primary and secondary weapons (e.g., assault rifles, sniper rifles, shotguns) and a suite of tactical gadgets. Your choices should complement your team's composition.
  • Key Gadgets: The Grappling Hook is vital for rapid mobility, scaling cliffs, and evading enemies. The Turret provides automated covering fire. Other gadgets like grenades, shields, or healing devices will dictate your role in the squad.
On the Ground: Tactics and Survival

Once deployed, the world is dynamic and dangerous. Your mission is not just to shoot, but to outsmart your enemy.

  • Move Like a Raider: Use the environment to your advantage. The grappling hook isn't just for travel—use it to gain high ground for a tactical advantage, swiftly flank enemies, or escape a deadly situation. Stay on the move; standing still is a death sentence.
  • Identify and Exploit Enemy Weak Points: ARC machines are heavily armored, but they have vulnerabilities. Scan your targets and aim for glowing weak points, power cores, or exposed joints to deal maximum damage.
  • Use Physics as a Weapon: The world is your arsenal. Kick objects to create distractions, use explosive barrels to trigger chain reactions, and leverage the environment to set up ambushes.
  • Ping System: Constant communication is key. Use the intuitive ping system to mark enemy locations, highlight resources, or point out strategic positions to your teammates without needing voice chat.
Engaging the ARC: Strategy Over Brute Force

You will encounter a variety of ARC enemies, from small scouts to towering behemoths.

  • Scout and Plan: Don't rush in blindly. Observe patrol routes and identify the types of enemies you're facing.
  • Divide and Conquer: In a squad, assign roles. Have one player draw aggro while others flank. Focus fire on a single high-priority target to eliminate threats quickly.
  • Adapt on the Fly: The ARC adapts. If a direct assault isn't working, change tactics. Fall back to a more defensible position, use hit-and-run tactics, or set up a kill zone with traps and turrets.
Teamwork is Non-Negotiable

This is the most important rule of being a Raider.

  • Revive and Support: When a teammate falls, prioritize their revival. Cover them while they get back up. Share resources and ammo.
  • Stick Together: A lone Raider is a dead Raider. There is strength in numbers. Coordinated fire and shared gadgets will overcome challenges that are impossible to face alone.
The Goal: Complete and Extract

Your mission isn't over until you've safely extracted.

  • Complete Dynamic Objectives: The world will present you with various tasks, from destroying key ARC assets to securing valuable intel. Work together to complete them.
  • Survive the Extraction: Once your main objective is complete, you must call for and secure an extraction point. This often triggers a final, intense wave of ARC forces. Hold the line, protect the zone, and ensure your entire squad makes it out alive to claim your rewards.
Become a Master Raider

Master the movement, learn the enemy patterns, communicate with your team, and creatively use every tool at your disposal. The ARC is a relentless foe, but with skill and cooperation, you can fight back and reclaim our world.
